A **Bates number** is a unique serial number stamped or printed on each page of a document set for legal cases.

**Example:**
If you have 1000 pages from various files, Bates numbers let you say “see Bates 563” and everyone knows the exact page in the pile.

**In simple words:**
A Bates number is like a page ID that helps people find and talk about the right document in legal cases.
